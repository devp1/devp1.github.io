Welcome.


Student. As a senior at Clarkstown High School South, I engage in coursework that explores Computer Science and all of its facets- from the principles of design to the execution of code.


Developer. I focus on projects that push the frontier of artificial intelligence and cloud computing. I am co-developing the framework behind a communicator for patients with ALS while also working with Stryker’s development team on its Ocular Monitor (which will be pushed into the industrial pipeline for autonomous surgical robots). In the past, I have migrated corporate infrastructure from legacy hardware to Amazon Web Service’s cloud environment, enhancing performance and reducing costs.


Social Activist. I work with the Electronic Frontier Foundation, the leading nonprofit organization in the fight for our digital civil liberties. As a youth member of the New York Civil Liberties Union and a Facilitator for the education and prevention program SAEDA (Student Activists Ending Dating Abuse), I also work to celebrate diversity, challenge social norms, and encourage my peers to combat gender-based violence.

My Recent Highlights.


In August and September 2018, I successfully obtained certifications as a:

Cloud Practitioner
Developer Associate
Solutions Architect Associate
Systems Operations Administrator Associate


Although candidates for certification exams must be 18 years or older, I reached out to Amazon’s CEO Jeff Bezos and made a case for why he should consider making an exception for me.
